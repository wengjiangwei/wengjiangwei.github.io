Welcome to Jiangwei Weng (翁江玮)'s Homepage.

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at PCALab@NJUST, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Jun Li.

I obtained M.S. degree in Computer Science in 2021 from Huzhou University, advised by Prof. Yunliang Jiang.

My current work focuses on image/video enhancement with its applications in real world. In the past, I have focused my reseach topics on fuzzy system and interpretable modeling for the epilepsy EEG recognition.
